About Us

Mehman is one of the leading online travel brand in Pakistan and offers customers tailor made holiday deals focusing on flights, hotels, visas and other holiday products. Customers can benefit from our online booking engine to book their holidays. Mehman is IATA accredited Agency since 2019 and registered with Security Exchange Commission of Pakistan since 2016.

Story of mehman starts when founders of mehman (Muhammad Aamer & Atiq Sarwar) booked their first umrah packages in 2015. It was realised how difficult is to buy umrah online. Travel agencies have poor customer services and don’t guide properly to umrah pilgrims. To serve people and help the travellers, mehman was born in 2016. Since July 2016, we have served many people and Alhamdulillah’s we have provided best customer service to all our customers.

We at Mehman.pk are very dedicated to providing best services you with all-inclusive Umrah packages, Hajj packages, Dubai visa, and cheap flight services.
