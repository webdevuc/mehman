Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 June 2021

Thank you for visiting our website (mehman.pk). Mehman.pk is trade name of Mehman Travel and Tours (private) limited. This document describe covers privacy policy of our website.

    This Privacy Policy covers:
  • What type of personal data we collect, use and how we collect it?
  • With whom and why we share your personal data?
  • What sort of choices you can make about the data we collect, use and share?

We may update this Policy to in response to changing laws, technical and business needs. To view our latest policy, visit this page when you need to review the privacy policy.

What sort of Personal Data do we collect?

When you create an account with us using sign up page, sign up to receive our newsletter or make a booking using our platform, you give us your personal information. We also collect other information through browser such as cookies placed on your browser when you visit our sites. We also receive information from our social media channels and our affiliated partners and other third party to improve our website and services.

Categories of personal information

    When you use our website or our services, we may collect the following kinds of personal information from you:
  • Name, email address, telephone number and addresses
  • Government-issued identification documents such as ID cards, passports required for bookings and identification.
  • Payment information such as bank account, debit and credit card number, expiry date and billing address
  • Travel preferences such as favourite destination, accommodation types and dietary requirements
  • Birth date and gender
  • Geolocation
  • Images, videos and other recordings
  • Social media accounts and the information you shared with us on these channels.
  • Communications with us such as recordings of calls with customer service representatives or emails to our associated business emails.
  • Internet searches, transactions and other interactions on our websites and apps
  • The searches and transactions carried out on our website and apps.
  • other people data you shared with us while making a booking.
  • Information we receive about you from our business partners and social media channels.
    When you install any of our apps or use our platform, cookies automatically collect the following types of information from your device:
  • IP address and Internet service provider
  • Device type
  • Internet browser type
  • Operating system
  • Mobile network
  • dates and times associated with your searches.
  • referring website and exit pages.

What are your rights and choices about your personal information?

You can use your data protection rights in various ways. You can unsubscribe from our marketing emails using unsubscribe buttons in our marketing emails. You can stop receiving latest news and offers by removing social media connections or unlinking our social media pages.

Who is responsible for Your Data?

Mehman Travels and Tours (Private) Limited has a Data Protection Officer who watches all the data of our platforms and processes related to data collection, storage and usage. In case you have any question regarding your data, our Data Protection Officer is available to answer all your questions you may have. You can use How to Contact us section to reach our Data Protection Officer. However, we may ask you to verify your identity before taking further action on your request.

Is your Personal data secure with us?

Data protection security for your data on our systems is our number one priority and we do take all reasonable steps to implement technical, physical and organisational measures.

Sharing your Personal Data

We may share your personal information with following third parties:

Travel suppliers. We share booking information with our travel suppliers such as hotels, airlines, car hire companies, insurance, holiday rental property owners. These suppliers may call you to verify your personal and booking details to confirm your identity and booking details.

Business partners. If we promote a product or offer a service in partnership with a third-party business partner, we will share your information with that partner to assist in marketing or to provide the associated product or service.

Third-party service providers. We share personal information with third parties for delivery of services to you and the operation of our business. For example, banks to process payments and software companies who provide us key software solutions to run our business.

Government Agencies. We may disclose your personal information to law enforcement agencies as permitted by law.

How to Contact us?

You can contact us using our phone numbers, sending us email, visiting our offices, using our social media channels or Contact us page on our website.
